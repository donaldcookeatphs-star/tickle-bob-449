The History of Anime

Anime started off in the early 1900's of japan.The first known animes were normally often short and silent films a good example of this is an anime called Namakura Gatana also known as the dull sword. During WW2 anime was used for propaganda this was from 1930's till the 1940's. Anime post war was a turning point in the industry as Osamu Tezuka had started animation this resulted into creating a back bone to modern day anime as Astro Boy came out in 1968 and was and is still possibly one of the most popular anime in the 1970s introduced the mecha genre and the 1980s was a golden age for animation it started getting a better budget and expermential films.
